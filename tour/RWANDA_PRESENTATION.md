## WHO IS ASHTON HALL?

Ashton Hall is one of the fastest-growing content creators and live streamers in the United States, with tens of millions of followers across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Snapchat. He has built an extraordinary global audience entirely organically — without relying on traditional celebrity collaborations or mainstream media.

His content captures raw, authentic moments and daily life experiences that resonate deeply with audiences worldwide, particularly across Africa where his fanbase is growing at an exceptional rate. He is not just a creator — he is a cultural phenomenon, with every video and livestream generating millions of views within hours of publishing.

Atouré Management & Consulting, the agency behind the IShowSpeed Africa Tour, has been mandated as the official on-ground agency for this tour.

---

## WHY AFRICA? WHY NOW?

Ashton Hall has identified Africa as one of his most engaged and fastest-growing audiences. This tour is his first ever visit to the continent — a historic moment that will be documented in real time across all his platforms.

The tour is built around a central storyline: the search for the "African Ashton Hall" — a talented young African creator who mirrors his archetype and will be discovered, elevated, and introduced to the world through this journey.

Every country on this tour will receive:
- Direct exposure to Ashton's global audience of tens of millions
- Authentic, organic content showcasing culture, people, and destinations
- Historic streaming moments — live, unfiltered, and widely shared
- A tangible legacy for local talent discovered or elevated along the way

## PROPOSED RWANDA LEG

**Proposed Duration:** 3 days

Rwanda represents a compelling addition to this tour — a country whose story of transformation, natural beauty, and modern ambition is exactly the kind of narrative that resonates with Ashton Hall's audience. A Rwanda leg would put the country in front of tens of millions of viewers who have never seen it the way its people live it.

---

## PROPOSED RWANDAN ITINERARY

**Day 1 — Arrival & Kigali Discovery**
- Arrival and welcome
- Kigali city tour — culture, markets, street life, local food
- Meeting with a local Rwandan content creator or young talent
- Evening live stream from Kigali

**Day 2 — Nature & Experience**
- Visit to Volcanoes National Park or Nyungwe Forest
- Gorilla trekking or wildlife experience — a landmark streaming moment
- Local village or community visit — authentic human story content
- Evening — cultural dinner, music, local personalities

**Day 3 — Legacy & Departure**
- Roundtable with young Rwandan creators/entrepreneurs
- Final content shoot — Kigali skyline, key landmarks
- Departure
